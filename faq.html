» What is the charge for same day delivery?..............

There is a flat fee of Rs 100.00 for same day delivery. Midnight delivery chagres are Rs 350.00 ( Selected Cities Only )

You will be able to review the delivery charges on the order Review Page before completing your order.

» What is my expected delivery time?

Deliveries are generally done at the preferred delivery time chosen by you , but this is not a fixed time of delivery . We try our best to delivery at the Preffered time chosen by you , but due to unavoidable circumstances , this may noy be possible as ALL our products are hand delivered and it is the Local florist who delivers the orders .

» What is the order cut off for same day delivery?

To have flowers delivered on the same day, Kindly place the order before 4 PM ( India Time )

» What happens if the recipient isn't available?

We have you covered!
When providing the delivery address, we ask for telephone numbers and we will co ordinate with the receiver to get the delivery done . We can either leave it by the door, on a verandah, by the garage or even with a neighbour.
Provide the instructions and so long as it's reasonable, our delivery drivers will do their very best to meet your request.
We hope you understand that we are no longer responsible once the order has been left at the designated premises.
Alternatively, if the recipient isn't available, our driver will attempt to phone them to schedule a suitable delivery time.
